YouTube Frame Search

Adds a search button to YouTube's video toolbar, allowing you to search the current frame with Google Lens instantly.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Use these shortcuts to search your screen with Google Lens:

  • Alt+Q: Capture and search the entire screen
  • Alt+W: Select and search a specific area

To set up shortcuts manually:

  1. Go to chrome://extensions/shortcuts
  2. Find "Screen Lens - Search Screen with Google Lens"
  3. Assign your preferred shortcut
